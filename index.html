Howdy!

I'm a Sound Designer from Belton, TX, currently living in Minneapolis, MN with my better half, Leo, and our two dogs. Thanks for visiting!

As a child, my mother (a recreational therapist) taught me the joy of making, and of play. As I got older, I became enamored with video games, and in high school I picked up a guitar and was introduced to Unity 3D. I completed a Bachelor of Science degree in Visualization from Texas A&M University, where I mingled with animators, game designers, programmers, and performance artists, eventually forging my own Sound Design path.

I went on to complete my MFA in Sound Design at the Savannah College of Art & Design, go from Intern to Team Member with Turn 10 Studios, and ship Forza Motorsport (2023), my first AAA title. Most recently I've joined Meta's Creative Audio Team, where I continue to cross-pollinate, find solutions, and find the joy in making play.

In my free time, I can be found playing video and board games, learning about history, watching Formula 1 racing or Minnesota Wild Hockey, making music for myself, cooking, or enjoying a beer (not mutually exclusive).

Some additional fun facts:

  • Yes, I do deliberately say Howdy as a greeting.
  • My dogs collectively have 3 eyes and 2 working ears.
  • Pineapple belongs on pizza. This isn't about me, this is a fact.
