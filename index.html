Howdy!

I'm a Sound Designer from Belton, TX, currently living in Minneapolis, MN with my better half, Leo, and our two dogs. Thanks for visiting!

I recently worked with Turn 10 Studios on Forza Motorsport, my first AAA title. Before that, I completed my MFA in Sound Design from the Savannah College of Art & Design and a Bachelor of Science degree in Visualization from Texas A&M University.

In my free time, I can be found playing video and board games, learning about history, watching Formula 1 racing or Minnesota Wild Hockey, learning to ice skate, making music for myself, cooking, or enjoying a beer (not mutually exclusive).

Some additional fun facts:

  • Yes, I do deliberately say Howdy as a greeting.
  • I start to sound like people I'm talking to within minutes.
  • My dogs collectively have 3 eyes and 2 working ears.
  • Pineapple belongs on pizza. This isn't about me, this is a fact.
